To choose a currency, tap the current currency name. Currencies are listed in alphabetical order by ISO 4217 currency code. The currency of your country is listed before any others.

To enter your wage, tap the current wage. On first launch, Wage Watch will attempt to fetch the minimum wage (converted to hourly USD if necessary) of your country.

To choose a pay period, tap the current pay period.

To start the stopwatch, tap Start. To pause the stopwatch, tap Stop. To reset the stopwatch, tap Reset.
